Our Story

Founded in 2009, Himalayan Delights has been serving authentic Nepali and Indian cuisine for over 15 years. What started as a small family restaurant has grown into one of Kathmandu's most beloved dining destinations.

Our master chefs bring generations of culinary expertise, using traditional recipes passed down through families. From hand-rolled momos to slow-cooked curries, every dish is prepared with passion and authentic spices sourced directly from local markets.

We believe in fresh, quality ingredients and maintaining the authentic taste that makes Nepali and Indian cuisine so special. Our commitment to excellence has earned us numerous awards and thousands of happy customers.
